Spent some time at UCSF, UCLA, and USC doing research in breast cancer (immunology), stem cell biology and etc. Now, currently at UChicago as a research tech studying the interplay between tumor microenvironment and cell behavior, with a specific interest in fibroblasts and wound healing.
