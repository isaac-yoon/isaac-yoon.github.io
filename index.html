About Me

Driven by my innate curiosity, I seek to understand - systems and the underlying patterns beneath stories. Whether it be the human body, mind, an algorithm, a compelling story, or whatever catches my attention, there is joy and elation to be had in understanding complex concepts and the relationships between seemingly disjointed parts. As a lifelong learner, I seek to make a meaningful impact wherever I go.

In my free time you can find me playing strategy games, learning choreography, reading, exploring restaurants, writing Yelp reviews, playing golf, honing my roundhouse kick, among many other things.
