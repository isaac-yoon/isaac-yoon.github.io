About Me

Driven by my innate curiosity, I seek to understand systems and the underlying patterns beneath stories. Whether it be the human body, mind, an algorithm, or a compelling story, I find joy in understanding complex concepts and the relationships between seemingly disjointed parts. I am a lifelong learner and hope to make a meaningful impact anywhere I go.

In my free time you can find me playing strategy games, learning choreography, reading, and exploring restaurants/writing Yelp reviews, among other things.
